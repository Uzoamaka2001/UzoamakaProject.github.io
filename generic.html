About Uzoamaka

Uzoamaka is an aspiring data analyst with several years in the banking sector over the years have interned and worked on a number of big data projects cut across a number of industries from the health sector analysis to Airline industry analysis. I also have imbibed tremendous soft skills like great team spirit, excellent communication skill, Emotional intelligence and ability to adapt and excel in every situation.

On a daily basis we make various decision, decision as to what to wear, what to eat and the best course of action in various scenatios but these decision are usually personal as businesses need to make these decisions but from an informed position. This is where data analysis comes to as we take these datas usually in their rawrest and dirtiest form, we clean these data and prepare them for analysis using various tools from Microsoft Excel, Power BI, SQL and even Tableau. This is used to glean data driven analysis for better decision making.
